#📚 Learning Module: Oxygen Evolution Reaction
An interactive learning module featuring knowledge and practice in material simulation with topic of Oxygen Evolution Reaction. Each pages are arranged as follow through research steps from beginning to end. In the end, this module will render scientific graphs publish-grade.

##💻 Technologies:
-QE
-ASE
-Streamlit
-VESTA
-Pandas

##🚀 Sylabus & Features
###Phase 1: Material Foundations
-Module 1: Bulk Selection & Variation
-Module 2: Slab Optimization & Surface Energy
-Module 3: Adsorbate Modeling & ZPE
###Phase 2: Thermodynamic & Kinetic Insights
-Module 4: Gibbs Free Energy Landscape
-Module 5: Overpotential Analysis
-Module 6: Microkinetic Modeling
-Module 7: Data Visualization - Volcano Graphs

##📃 The Background
One of research topic in surface science is catays.
This module stands out by offering a holistic and integrative learning experience that bridges established theories and emerging advancements.
